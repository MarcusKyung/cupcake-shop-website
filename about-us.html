Rose City Cupcakes

Portland's Best Cupcakes!

Welcome to Rose City Cupcakes located in the heart of Portland, Oregon! Our charming cupcake shop is nestled in a historic building, and is the perfect place to indulge in some sweet treats. Our cupcakes are made from scratch using only the finest and freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our talented bakers take great pride in creating unique and delicious flavor combinations that are sure to delight your taste buds. At Rose City Cupcakes, we offer a variety of cupcakes to suit every taste preference, from classic flavors like vanilla and chocolate, to more adventurous flavors like lavender honey and chai spice. We also have gluten-free and vegan options available, so everyone can enjoy a sweet treat.

Our cozy and inviting shop is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a cupcake with a cup of locally roasted coffee or a refreshing glass of iced tea. And if you're looking for a special gift, we also offer custom cupcake orders for events and celebrations. Stop by Rose City Cupcakes and treat yourself to a little something sweet today!
